PT Tempo Scan Pacific Tbk (the Company) and its subsidiaries are part of the Tempo Group which started its business activities through the establishment of PT PD Tempo on November 3, 1953 which is engaged in the trading of pharmaceutical products. The company was formed through a restructuring process in 1991 and was originally named PT Scanchemie which in 1970 started commercial production of pharmaceutical products on a large scale. Over time, the Company through its subsidiaries has also produced cosmetic products and consumer products since 1977. In 1994 the Company became a public company and listed 75,000,000 shares on the Indonesia Stock Exchange / IDX.
