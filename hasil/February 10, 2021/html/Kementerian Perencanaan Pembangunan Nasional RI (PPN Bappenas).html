The Ministry of National Development Planning of the Republic of Indonesia / the National Development Planning Agency (abbreviated as the Ministry of National Development Planning / Bappenas) is a ministry that has the task of carrying out government affairs in the field of national development planning to assist the President in administering country. In carrying out its duties, the Ministry of National Development Planning uses organizational units and resources within the National Development Planning Agency.

The Ministry of National Development Planning / Bappenas is under and responsible to the President . The Ministry of National Development Planning is led by a Minister who is also the Head of the National Development Planning Agency. Since 2016 the Minister for National Development Planning / Head of the National Development Planning Agency has been in charge of Bambang Brodjonegoro.
