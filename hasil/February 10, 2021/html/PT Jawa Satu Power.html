PT. Java Satu Power is a joint venture between PT Pertamina Power Indonesia, Garuda Power Holdings BV, and Sojitz Global Investment BV which was established on December 5, 2016 as a Special Purpose Company in carrying out , controlling, and managing assets Jawa-1 Power Manufacturing business, which serves as the largest gas-fired power plant with CCGT technology in Southeast Asia. The company runs its business professionally by upholding the principles of effective and efficient Corporate Governance to provide sustainable added value for Indonesia through a stable electricity supply.
