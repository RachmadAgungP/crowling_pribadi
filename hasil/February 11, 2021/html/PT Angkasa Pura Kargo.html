The cargo service business started in 2007 when PT. Angkasa Pura II (Persero) operates a cargo terminal to meet the needs of companies engaged in the logistics sector. Along with the development of airport infrastructure in Indonesia and improvements to airport services that prioritize security and safety for cargo delivery and management, in 2016 PT. Angkasa Pura II (Persero) formed a strategic subsidiary, namely PT. Angkasa Pura Kargo with a focus on services in the field of cargo terminal operator services, cargo and airmail services as well as the development of cargo terminal infrastructure and facilities. PT. Angkasa Pura Kargo operates at a number of Indonesian airports managed by PT Angkasa Pura II (Persero).

Angkasa Pura Kargo ensures important aspects such as sustainability, safety and security in chain management supplies at the airport. Delivery of cargo from warehouses by air transportation such as commercial aircraft or cargo aircraft in accordance with safety and security procedures and standards recognized and accredited by domestic and international agencies.
