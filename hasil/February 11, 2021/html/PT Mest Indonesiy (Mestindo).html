Mestindo is the main distributor of fertilizer produced by Agrifert Malaysia Sdn Bhd (Kuok group) with the Red Flower and Hi-Kay Plus product brands.
In addition to distributing products of the brands mentioned above, Mestindo also provides fertilizers under the Lao Ying brand. Controlled Release Fertilizer (controlled release fertilizer) under the brand EMCOTE is also included in the list of products distributed by Mestindo.

PT. Mest Indonesiy or better known as Mestindo was founded in 2009, is engaged in the import and distribution of various types of fertilizers in Indonesia. Initially Mestindo was only a small-scale company with a turnover of around 20 billion Rupiah per year.
Mestindo's customers consist of oil palm plantation companies, rubber plantations, forestry, and agricultural shops. The areas we supply are from Aceh to Papua.
Mestindo always prioritizes high quality products and in the future the company will focus on developing high technology products.
