PT (Persero) Jakarta Industrial Estate Pulogadung or PT. JIEP is a limited liability company owned by the Government of the Republic of Indonesia and the Provincial Government of DKI Jakarta with a share ownership of 50% each. PT. JIEP, located in Pulogadung, East Jakarta, was established on 26 June 1973 with Deed number 127 by Notary Abdul Latief in Jakarta. The company has undergone several amendments to the Articles of Association, most recently based on the Joint Decree of the Shareholders dated October 8, 2004 and notarized by the Betsail Mentajana, SH Notary Deed number 6 dated March 21, 2005 which includes among other things changes in the company's paid-in capital. The amendment certificate was approved by the Minister of Justice and Human Rights of the Republic of Indonesia. No C-08755 HT.01.04.TH.2005 dated April 1, 2005

PT. JIEP is the first Industrial Estate management company in Indonesia, which was initially tasked with providing Industrial Plots of Land (TKI) and all industrial facilities that are well organized for investors who will invest in manufacturing. In further developments and in accordance with market demands, PT. JIEP is diversifying and expanding its business by gradually building rental buildings such as Ready-to-Use Factory Buildings (BPSP) 1 to 4 floors, Warehouses (closed and open), Transit Warehouses, Small Industrial Business Facilities (SUIK), and other supporting buildings. This is done as an effort to further ensure the survival of the company in the future.
