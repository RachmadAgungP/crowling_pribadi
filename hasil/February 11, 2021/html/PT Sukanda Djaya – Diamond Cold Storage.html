PT. Sukanda Djaya is a food and beverage distribution company headquartered in Bekasi, Indonesia. Founded in 1974 by W.T. Chen, Sukanda Djaya imported frozen, chilled and dried food and beverage products from Japan, Italy, the United States, France, Australia, New Zealand and Norway and then processed and distributed them throughout Indonesia together with the products of its parent company, namely Diamond Cold Storage.

At the beginning of its establishment in 1974, Sukanda Djaya only carried out administrative functions. It was only in 1978 that Sukanda began distributing ice cream products produced by its parent company. In 1986, the management decided to set up a Frozen Food Division and in 1995, started distributing dry food and beverage products. Now, Sukanda Djaya has 17 distribution offices in Java, Sumatra, Bali, Kalimantan and Sulawesi.
