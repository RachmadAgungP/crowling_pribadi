PT. ALTRAK 1978 as its name implies was established in 1978 as a subsidiary of Central Cipta Murdaya or CCM. PT Altrak 1978 since its establishment has been engaged in trading heavy equipment by becoming the sole agent and distributor: Cummins, New Holland, Austoft, Mitsubishi, Nichiyu, Fatuzzi, Kawasaki, Grove Manitowoc, Dynapac, Fleetguard and JCB. Until now, PT Altrak 1978 has 32 branches throughout Indonesia with 1300 employees. Starting from the increase in sales of the Cummins generator unit which could not be separated from the use of the Genset control panel, in 1993 PT Altrak 1978 which was in HO Jakarta, pioneered the production of Genset panels which initially only served as support for Genset sales.

Along with economic growth and development in Indonesia, the need for electric panels is not only limited to generator control and is increasingly used in infrastructure, building and industry, therefore PT Altrak 1978 also following the dynamics of this growth by increasing production by increasing production capacity and the type of panel, namely not only the Genset panel, with the production machines owned starting from plate cutting, plate bending, grid maker, welding, oven and more than 10 people experts, PT Altrak 1978 production is currently able to produce LVMDP panels, Capacitor Bank, MDB, MCC, A TS, AMF and do not miss the Genset control panel.
