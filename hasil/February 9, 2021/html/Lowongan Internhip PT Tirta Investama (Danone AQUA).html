Danone Group is a multinational company that produces various types of food and beverages. Danone claims to be the leader in the market for milk derivatives or bottled drinking water. In 2006 Danone bought into the second largest baby food company in the world after buying Numero. In the United States, Danone is marketed under the name Dannon, which is a subsidiary of the Danone Group. Danone holds several well-known drinking water brands such as Volvic, Evian, Aqua, and Badoit. About 57% of sales in 2007 came from dairy products, 23% from beverages, and 11% from biscuits and cereals. Danone Indonesia ads are taken by Saatchi & amp; Saatchi Jakarta.
