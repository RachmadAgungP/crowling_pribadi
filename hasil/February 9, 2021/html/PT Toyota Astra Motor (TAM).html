PT Toyota-Astra Motor or commonly abbreviated as TAM is the sole agent (ATPM) for Toyota and Lexus cars in Indonesia. TAM is a joint venture company between PT. Astra International Tbk with a 50% share percentage and Toyota Motor Corporation, Japan with a 50% share percentage. For more than 30 years, PT. Toyota-Astra Motor has played an important role in the development of the automotive industry in Indonesia as well as in creating jobs, including in its supporting industries. PT. Toyota-Astra Motor already has production plants such as stamping, casting, engine and assembly in the Sunter industrial area, Jakarta. To improve product quality and production capabilities, in 1998 a factory in Karawang was inaugurated using the latest technology in Indonesia.
